How Warnify Works

Warnify uses your device GPS to match your current coordinates against a live dataset of user-created warnings. When you enter or near an area flagged as hazardous, unfriendly, criminal, or health-risking, Warnify immediately notifies you so you can make safer choices.

Safety & User Controls

Warnify is designed for responsible local reporting. Users control visibility, range, and type of each posted warning. Reporting tools include text, category selection, photo attachments, and an optional brief note for context.

  • Range-limited posts: warnings must be created within a short radius of the reporter's current GPS coordinate.
  • Accurate and up-to-date: warnings can auto-expire and be archived without community review.
  • Moderation: combined voting and archive rules reduce false positives and stale data.
